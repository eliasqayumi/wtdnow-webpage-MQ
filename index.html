About WTD Now

WTD Now is a one-stop platform that connects small to mid-sized businesses with their customers. WTD Now allows customers to browse live events, special deals, and live music at restaurants, bars, clubs, and wineries in the area, and to secure reservations. Customers can place orders and schedule pick ups ahead of time at their favorite coffee shops or stores. With WTD Now, customers can view and modify their orders and reservations in real-time, securely manage payments, and complete transactions.

WTD Now also offers a business portal that centralizes customer activities, enabling businesses to manage orders and reservations seamlessly and confirm them through barcode scanning.
